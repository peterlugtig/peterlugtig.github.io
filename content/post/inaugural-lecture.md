On 2 april 2026 I delivered my inaugural speech as professor data quality at the [dept. of Methods and Statistics](https://www.uu.nl/en/organisation/methodology-and-statistics).
The inaugural lecture is a Dutch tradition: the idea is that you deliver a lecture that is understandable for the general public, and sets out what the scope of the professorship is, and what you intend to do.

If you want to read what I said, and thereby also learn a bit more about what I am doing, and am intending to do to make this world a tiny bit of better place you can here read the [original Dutch delivery of my speech](files/static/tekst_oratie_datakwaliteit_webversie.pdf) or read [the english translation of my lecture here](files/static/text_inaugural_lecture_data_quality.pdf).

A more formal plan for the chair can [still be found here](files/static/profile-report-chair-data-quality-online-version.pdf)
